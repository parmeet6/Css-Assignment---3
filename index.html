About Reese's Peanut chocolates

Reese's Peanut Butter Cups are a popular candy consisting of a chocolate cup filled with peanut butter. They were created by H.B. Reese, a former dairy farmer and shipping foreman for Milton S. Hershey. Reese's Peanut Butter Cups were first introduced to the market in 1928. Did you know that about 36 million Reese's Peanut Butter Cups are produced every day?
